The Best Avocado Toast

Link to the official recipe

Description:

This avocado toast recipe is a simple and delicious way to enjoy the creamy goodness of avocados on toasted bread. It's perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a light snack!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices of bread (your choice: sourdough, whole grain, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, radishes, red pepper flakes, feta cheese, or poached eggs

Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of bread until golden brown and crispy.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocado and mash it with a fork until creamy but still slightly chunky.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.
  5. Add any optional toppings you desire.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious avocado toast!
