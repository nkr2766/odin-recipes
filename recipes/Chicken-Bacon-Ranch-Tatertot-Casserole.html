Recipe for a killer casserole

Link to the official recipe

Description:

This Chicken Bacon Ranch Tater Tot Casserole is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that combines crispy tater tots, tender chicken, crispy bacon, and creamy ranch dressing. It's perfect for a weeknight dinner or a potluck gathering!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound frozen tater tots
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, crumbled bacon, ranch dressing, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Spread the tater tots evenly in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Pour the chicken and bacon mixture over the tater tots, spreading it evenly.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the tater tots are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired.
  8. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Bacon Ranch Tater Tot Casserole!
